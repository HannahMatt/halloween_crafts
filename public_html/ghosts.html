Hanging Ghosts

These cute ghosts can be hung from anywhere to instantly make a room, hallway, or yard feel a bit spookier. We used tissues for ours, but feel free to use tissue paper or trash bags to make the ghosts even bigger!

What you will need:

  • Sewing Needle and Thread - $3.00
  • Black Sharpie - $2.50
  • Tissues - $0.99
  • Scissors - $7.00
  • Printer Paper - $0.10
  • Total Price = $13.59

Instructions:

  1. Crumple paper into a loose ball.
  2. Place one tissue over it.
  3. Tie the thread around the base of the paper ball. * Not too tightly though, or the tissue will tear!
  4. Using your Sharpie, draw a ghostly face on the tissue.
  5. Thread the sewing needle and pull through the top of the head so you can hang it anywhere.
