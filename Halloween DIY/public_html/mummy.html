Cute Mummy Candles

These candles light up any living area, while creating a festive space to celebrate Halloween.

What you will need:

  • Mason Jars
  • Toilet Paper
  • Googly Eyes
  • Tape
  • Tea Candles

Instructions:

  1. Wrap mason jars in toilet paper, tape end to secure in place.
  2. Tape/glue googly eyes to toilet paper in desired placement.
  3. Place candle in jar.
  4. Light the candle and turn off the lights to see your mummies glow!
