Halloween Banner

What you will need:

  • Construction Paper Orange and Black - $2.00
  • Letter Stickers - $0.50
  • Ribbon - $0.25
  • Tape - $1.00
  • Scissors - $7.00
  • Decorative Stickers - $0.75
  • Total Price = $11.50

Instructions:

  1. Cut out equal size triangles from construction paper.
  2. Place and center letters on the paper triangles.
  3. Measure out ribbon to the length you need depending on how many letters you make.
  4. Punch holes in the top of the construction paper to feed ribbon throug for hanging.
  5. After stringing all letters on ribbon, place on wall and tape in desired location.
